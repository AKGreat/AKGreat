Who are we?

The Walking Club of Malta(WCM) was setup by a local group of friends on Facebook that love to explore and preserve the islands natural landscape. The first meeting that was setup was done wayback in 2015. The turnout was spectacular, with around 200 people that came to the cottonera bastions to experience this. So obviously we decided continue theses type of meetings and we are still holding them till this day.

Our Aim

The aims of our organisation are enshrined in our statute, which mentions the following five main goals:

  • To develop and protect the natural beauty and cultural heritage to be found in the countryside of the Maltese Islands.
  • To establish and to preserve the right of access to the open countryside and to the coastal zones.
  • To identify and to ensure the maintenance of a network of public footpaths in the open countryside.
  • To promote rambling activities as a means of education on the Maltese natural and cultural endowment and as a means of a healthy lifestyle.
  • To organise walks and other educational activities and to disseminate among civil society information leading to a greater respect of the countryside.
