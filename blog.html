Mtarfa

Duration: 3-4 hours

An easy walk starting at the Mdina Gate from where we head towards Mtarfa where we will pass through the old garrison town with its military buildings and pass close to a very old chapel overlooking the Chadwick Lakes. On leaving Mtarfa, we then head to Tal- Kanċillier olive press where we can watch olive oil being processed. There will be olive oil for sale for those who wish to purchase some.

Burmarrad

Duration: 3-4 hours

The rural village of Burmarrad is the site of BirdPark Malta, home to flamingos and storks, and there are hiking trails through verdant Għajn Rihana valley in Wied ta’ Għajn Mula country park. Open occasionally for tours, San Pawl Milqi archaeological site has prehistoric ruins and remains of a Roman-era villa. Built in 1983, the Parish Church sits on an elegant piazza, with cafes and fashion stores in quiet streets.
